About Us

Midnight pharmacy is known for providing excellent care and customer service to meet individuals needs till midnight 7 days a week. We currently have two branches; one is in Nottingham and one is in Bradford. Our first branch was opened in Bradford in 2009. We quickly expanded and in 2010 we opened a second branch in Nottingham. We provide a range of different kind of services to suit individuals and care home needs. Please see our services page for more information.

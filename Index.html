Welcome to Midnight Pharmacy

Midnight Pharmacy is Nottingham's Late Night pharmacy, offering pharmacy products and prescription dispensing services to the public across Nottingham. Based at 194 Alfreton Road, Nottingham, NG7 3PE. Open from 9am to midnight, we offer prescription collection and delivery services from all doctors surgeries across Nottingham in NG1, NG2, NG3, NG4, NG5, NG6, NG7, NG8, NG9, NG10, NG11, NG12, NG13, NG14, NG15, NG16, NG17, NG18, NG19, NG20, NG21, NG22, NG23, NG24, NG25.


We can collect your prescriptions and deliver your medication to a location to suit you, whether that's at work, or to your house.

At the pharmacy We also offer a range of other services, including providing dispensing and training services for care homes, compliance and nomad blister packs for patients in the community, MDS systems, including the Boots MDS system and Nomad systems along with fully colour coded MAR charts.

Midnight Pharmacy also provides a minor ailment service as an NHS service - Pharmacy First allows you to come to the pharmacy and receive medication without having to see the GP and get a prescription, for various minor ailments that can be treated with over the counter medication.


We also provide NHS seasonal flu vaccination service for at risk patients - why wait to see the GP - pop into to us at your convenience and have your free jab. Emergency contraception, travel vaccination clinic, Hepatitis B vaccination, Yellow Fever Vaccination, Rabies Vaccination. Private services for erectile dysfunction, and hair loss also available. A large range of private and NHS healthcare services available seven days a week to midnight.


We also have over 8000 items available for purchase on our website with big discounts and better prices than supermarkets and other online pharmacies.

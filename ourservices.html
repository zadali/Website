Our services

We offer a range of services in our pharmacies. NHS and private services are offered.

Click on the buttons inside the tabbed menu:

Professional Pharmacy Services

Care Home Services
We offer a comprehensive dispensing, training, and pharmacist support service for care homes seven days a week to midnight. (Also see attached document)

Community Dossette Service
We can package your medication into a range of compliance aids to help you to remember to take your medicines at the right time.

Free prescription delivery service
We can deliver your prescription free of charge to any location in Nottinghamshire (delivery outside of nottingham may be chargable)

Medicines Use Reviews
We are able to offer you an annual medicine check-up to ensure you are taking your prescribed medication correctly. Our expert pharmacists will then speak to your GP if any changes need to be made to your treatments.

NHS prescription collection service
We can pick up your prescriptions from your surgery and have it ready for you to collect at a time suitable to you.

NHS prescription dispensing service
We can dispense any NHS prescriptions we pick up from your surgery or you bring to us, or send to us.

Substance Misuse Services
Supervision of consumption of methadone and subutex for patients being treated for substance misuse, service runs to midnight seven days a week.

Retail Pharmacy Services

Blood pressure checking service
Pop in to have your blood pressure checked for free - no appointment needed.

Cholesterol testing services
Cholesterol testing service available - a charge may be payable

Diabetes testing service
Pop in to have your blood glucose measured for free - no appointment needed.

Erectile Dysfunction medication
We provide a full Erectile Dysfunction clinic and can provide prescription strength medication after a consultation with our pharmacist.

Hajj and Umrah Vaccination
Private vaccination service for pilgrims going on the Hajj or Umrah. Meeting the visa requirements of the Saudi government for Meningitis ACWY vaccination.

Malaria Clinic
We can supply prescription medicines for prevention of malaria - no appointment or prescription needed.

Private Prescription service
We can dispense private prescriptions at very competitive rates

Private Stop Smoking Service
Private stop smoking service also available with prescription strength medication, subject to consultation with our pharmacist

Travel Vaccination Clinic
We offer a comprehensive travel vaccination service, including yellow fever vaccine, Hepatitis B, Typhoid, Rabies and many others.

Weight Loss Clinic
Prescription strength medicines available to support you in your weight loss targets. Subject to consultation with our pharmacist.

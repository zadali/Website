Our services

We offer a range of services in our pharmacies. NHS and private services are offered.

  • Dispensing of NHS prescriptions
  • Free Prescription collection and delivery services
  • Substance misuse services – supervised consumption of methadone and subutex
  • Ailments schemes – pharmacy first
  • Care Home Dispensing services
  • Emergency hormonal contraception
  • Private Services
  • private consultations for Erectile dysfunction and supply of prescription medication
  • Flu Vaccination
  • Full Travel Vaccination in conjunction with MASTA
  • Hajj and Umrah vaccinations
  • Blood pressure testing
  • Blood glucose measurements
  • Blood cholesterol measurements
  • Health Advice
